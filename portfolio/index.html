About Me

While earning an Associate of Science in Mechanical Drafting and CAD from Pikes Peak State College, I've taken on the dynamic roles of VP of Sales / Product Manager at Borealis and Terrene Tires. My core competencies are driving eCommerce and B2B sales, leading product development, and process improvement that scale success for small businesses.

At these organizations, our teams have consistently surpassed growth targets, achieving record revenue spikes and gross margin increases. With a focus on strategic sales initiatives and product launches, like the Keystone Electric Bike, we've seen a significant lift in average order values.

My goal is to continue aligning with the company's culture, bringing diverse perspectives and experiences to foster an environment of innovation and growth.
