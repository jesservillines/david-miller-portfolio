About Me

While earning an Associate of Science in Mechanical Drafting and CAD from Pikes Peak State College, I've taken on the dynamic roles of VP of Sales / Product Manager at Borealis and Terrene Tires. My goal is to continue aligning with the company's culture, bringing diverse perspectives and experiences to foster an environment of innovation and growth.
